Overview

SecurePass Studio provides local password strength checks and a generator. It runs inside your browser and is provided for convenience only.

Responsible Use

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
  • Do not rely on the score as a guarantee of security.
  • Prefer a trusted password manager to store credentials.

Security Guidance

Avoid testing real passwords on shared or public devices. Generated passwords are suggestions only; you are responsible for how you use and store them.

Limitations

Strength results are estimates based on common patterns and entropy. Actual resistance to attacks depends on many factors outside this tool’s control.
